Latitude and Max Temperature

This graph I found particularily interesting because it supports some interesting science. It can be seen that there is a rise in temperature for areas closer to the equator. The latitude with the greatest average max temperature seems to be in the southern hemisphere. Also to note, the temperatures on average are greater in the southern hemisphere than in the northern hempishphere.

This is not because the southern hemisphere has higher temperatures than the northern hemisphere but has to do with when the data was taken. The data was taken in January 2017. In January the southern hemisphere is always in Summer while the northern hemisphere is in Winter. This is due to the tilt of the Earth on its axis as it rotates around the Sun. In January the southern hemispher is pointed more towards the Sun than the Northern.
